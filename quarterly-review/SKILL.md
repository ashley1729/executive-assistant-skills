# Quarterly Review

Prepare a comprehensive quarterly review of goals, metrics, and priorities.

## Steps

1. Review goals set at the start of the quarter:
   - completed
   - in progress
   - abandoned
   - missed
2. Gather key metrics:
   - revenue or financial performance if available
   - productivity metrics: tasks completed, sessions logged, content shipped
   - health or habit metrics if tracked
3. Highlight wins:
   - what went well
   - why it worked
4. Identify misses:
   - what did not happen
   - likely root cause
5. Surface patterns:
   - recurring blockers
   - consistently strong areas
   - emerging trends
6. Draft next quarter priorities:
   - top 3 goals with measurable targets
   - key risks to watch
   - one habit to start, one to stop, one to continue
7. Format as a one-page executive brief.

## Output format

```markdown
# Quarterly Review — <quarter>

## Executive summary
...

## Goals
| Goal | Status | Evidence | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|

## Wins
- ...

## Misses / lessons
- ...

## Patterns
- ...

## Next quarter priorities
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...
```
