# Weekly Competitor Scan

Research the latest developments from competitors and the broader AI assistant market.

## Scope

Search for news about:

- personal AI assistants
- AI productivity tools
- agent frameworks
- executive assistant automation
- inbox/calendar/task automation

## Focus

- What are competitors doing that we should learn from?
- What are they missing that we do well?
- What new product launches, feature updates, or funding announcements matter?

## Output format

Keep it to one page. Cite all sources with URLs.

```markdown
# Weekly Competitor Scan — <date>

## Market Moves
- <what happened> — <source>

## Opportunities
- <gap we could fill>

## Threats
- <thing that could affect positioning>

## Recommended moves
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...
```
